# CoSE Pulse Vision

## Mission

No student should miss an opportunity because they didn't know it existed.

---

## Purpose

CoSE Pulse is an intelligent information platform that continuously monitors official College of Science & Engineering resources, extracts valuable information, validates it, and distributes it in formats that benefit students, faculty, and administrators.

---

## Long-Term Vision

CoSE Pulse will become the digital information hub for the College of Science & Engineering.

Instead of manually searching dozens of websites, students and representatives receive timely, accurate, and actionable information automatically.
