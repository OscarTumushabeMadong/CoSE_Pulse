# CoSE Pulse

An intelligent automation platform for the College of Science & Engineering at San Francisco State University.

---

## Overview

CoSE Pulse automatically discovers, validates, organizes, and monitors official College of Science & Engineering information.

Originally built as a faculty and staff contact scraper, the platform is evolving into an AI-powered information assistant for:

- Faculty & Staff Contacts
- Student Opportunities
- Scholarships
- Internships
- Events
- Workshops
- Research Opportunities
- Career Fairs
- News & Announcements
